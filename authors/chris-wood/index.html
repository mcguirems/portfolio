Chris Wood (1)

Coming from a racing and road background, Chris has logged countless hours on adventure and 24 hour races and now spends his time seeking MTB bliss.  A genuine team player and all-around nice guy, he truly understands what crossover riders need to learn to fully embrace off-road riding.  He still rides road but feels the call of the bigger and gnarlier trails every spring.  Chris loves to guide people into new territory and skills and is always excited to be there when somebody gets it

He is based in Ottawa, Canada
