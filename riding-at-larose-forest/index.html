Riding at Larose Forest

Mike McGuire
October 16, 2018
Updated on November 3, 2023

Larose Forest near Limoges, Ontraio is purpose built bike singletrack with combinations of loops adding up to just over 24km. The terrain is sandy and flat with nice flow, steep drop cuts along the creek sections and nicely bermed corners all around.  Keep your eyes peeled for some figure 8 loops and side lines that spice it up even more. Fun but not boring for all rider levels, especially on a fatbike. Everything is 2-way and there are other users including, hikers, runners, dog mushers and deer. Remember to be nice to everyone.
